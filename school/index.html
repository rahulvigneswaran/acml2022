    Online Asian Machine Learning School

    The Online Asian Machine Learning School (OAMLS) aims to help prepare the next generation machine learning researchers and practitioners. It will be held on November 8 - 19, 2022.

    Photo by Scott Graham on Unsplash

    The Online Asian Machine Learning School (OAMLS) aims to help prepare the next generation machine learning researchers and practitioners by providing them with knowledge of machine learning fundamentals as well as state-of-the-art advances. It focuses on participants in the Asia-Pacific region; the virtual format, supported by ever improving communication technologies, allows affordable participation from students and practitioners from a large part of the region, including those from under-represented areas, who may otherwise be unable to afford travel to a physical international school.

    The School is part of the Asian Conference on Machine Learning. It will be held on November 8 - 19, 2022.

    Why an online school? Physical schools have their advantages and our online school is not meant to replace them. However, international travel is required to attend the physical schools, limiting participation, particularly from those in regions not well represented in machine learning. Communication technologies have improved so much that we believe that an effective online school is possible. We aim to provide an interactive school, to build some of the bonds among participants that you would get in a physical school.
